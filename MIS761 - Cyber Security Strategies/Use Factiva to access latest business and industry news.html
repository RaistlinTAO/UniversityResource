Factiva provides access to the latest business and industry news. The database allows users to browse through a collection of global content sources - including newspapers, continuously updated newswires from Dow Jones and Reuters and The Associated Press, as well as journals and magazines, websites, blogs, and multimedia.

1. Access the Factiva Database using Deakin SSO (your student credential) 2. Type in the headline of the article in the "Search From" box, you may need to change the data range in "Date" if the article of interest was published 3 months ago. For example, search the article "Microsoft Grilled on Capitol Hill Over Security Failures". You may need to scroll down and find the one with the same headline. 3. Clicking the link will lead to the following article page. It shows metadata by default. You can access the full article by changing the Display option to "Full Article"

4. Have fun and enjoy your reading. If you need further assistance, please contact the librarian from Deakin Library.
