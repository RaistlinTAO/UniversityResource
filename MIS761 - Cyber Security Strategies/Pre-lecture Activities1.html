Tip for Skimming Research Papers

  1. Identify the Topic:

    • Read the title and abstract to grasp the main focus.

  2. Skim Key Sections:

    • Introduction: For background and purpose.
    • Discussion/Practical Implications: For real-world relevance.
    • Conclusion: For summarized findings.
